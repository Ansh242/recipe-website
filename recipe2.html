Murgh Makhani (Butter Chicken)

Prep Time: 30 Mins (plus marination) Cook Time: 40 Mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Marinade: 1 lb boneless chicken thighs (diced), 1/2 cup plain yogurt, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp Kashmiri chili powder, 1 tsp garam masala.
  • Gravy: 2 tbsp butter, 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1.5 cups tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
  • Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix the diced chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes (overnight is best).
  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat a pan over medium-high heat with a little oil. Sear the marinated chicken pieces until browned and cooked through. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Start the Gravy: In the same pan, melt the butter. Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Build the Sauce: Stir in the cumin powder, coriander powder, and Kashmiri chili powder. Pour in the tomato puree and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes until the oil starts to separate from the sauce.
  5. Blend (Optional): For an ultra-smooth restaurant-style gravy, let the sauce cool slightly and blend it until smooth, then return it to the pan.
  6. Final Simmer: Add the cooked chicken back into the sauce. Stir in the heavy cream and crush the dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi) between your palms into the pan. Simmer for 5 more minutes and serve hot with naan.
