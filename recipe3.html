Authentic Palak Paneer

Prep Time: 15 Mins Cook Time: 25 Mins Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 large bunch fresh spinach (palak), washed
  • 200g Paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream or fresh cream
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Blanch the Spinach: Boil water in a large pot. Add the washed spinach leaves and blanch for 2 minutes. Immediately transfer the leaves to a bowl of ice water to retain their bright green color.
  2. Puree the Spinach: Drain the spinach and transfer it to a blender along with the green chili. Blend into a smooth puree and set aside.
  3. Pan-fry the Paneer (Optional): Lightly pan-fry the paneer cubes in a little oil until golden. Soak them in warm water to keep them soft while you make the gravy.
  4. Make the Base: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add the tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the mixture thickens and oil leaves the sides of the pan.
  6. Combine and Simmer: Pour in the spinach puree and mix well. Add the paneer cubes. Simmer gently for 5 minutes. Finish by stirring in the heavy cream. Serve hot with roti or jeera rice.
